News Feed

New Courses - 3rd November

Some brand new courses have been added to our training program, These courses include First aid training by professionals, and also a advance course, this is to push the boundary and make you feel like you can take on any water, no matter how rough, check out our training page to find out more.

Guest Visit - 22nd October

There has been some rumours floating around out club house that one of cannoning legends may on site soon, Well we are now glad to let the cat out the bag, we are proud to announce that 2012 London Gold medalist Ed McKeever will be on site and taking part in activities for all members.

Halloween special - 20th October

Don't forget that on the 31st of October (Halloween) we will be hosting loads of 'spooky' events for all ages, fancy dress, sweets, party games and best dress competitions will all be held, no need to sign up if your a member, just show up in your Halloween costume and join the fun.

Sponsored Canoe ride - 10th October

Join us as we raise money for Nobel causes by riding our canoes, loads of activity's to take place to help raise money for cancer, There will be mini-games and larger rowing competitions will all proceeds going to charity, come down before to get registered and raise some money for those who need it, everyone invited to our event open to all members and non-members for this Nobel cause.
