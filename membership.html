FAQ

Q:

How Long does the membership last?

A:

membership lasts for 12 months from the date you join. We do not supply day membership/licences

Q:

Does everyone in the boat need to be a member?

A:

The principle of the licence is that the canoe/kayak which a member is paddling is licensed on the water covered in the scheme so long as the member is actually in the canoe/kayak. The member may take non-members passengers; however the canoe is unlicensed, if the member is not on board. Passengers do not have third party insurance.

Q:

Do I still need a licence for a inflatable canoe/kayak?

A:

Yes you would still need a licence, if one is needed, in an inflatable canoe or kayak

Q:

How long will it take for my card to arrive?

A:

Your membership card will arrive between 5 to 10 working days.
