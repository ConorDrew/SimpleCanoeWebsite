Training

Training for you, about you

About Training

Training will be held on our waters, you will be trained by professionals in small groups to give you the most out of training, our experts will answer any questions you have on the day and after the training is complete you will receive a recognised qualification in that field.

Membership Benefits

All training courses will be availabe even to non-members, Members will get courses at a discounted price. anyone on the course will get to use the clubs waters and equipment while on the training program.
